## Idea 1: "Neon Arena" (Cyberpunk Sports)

*   **Design Movement**: Cyberpunk / High-Tech Sports Broadcast
*   **Core Principles**:
    *   **Energy & Speed**: Visuals should convey motion and intensity.
    *   **Data-Driven**: Emphasize stats and numbers with monospaced fonts and technical lines.
    *   **Immersive Dark Mode**: Deep blacks with vibrant neon accents to make content pop.
*   **Color Philosophy**:
    *   Background: Deep Charcoal / Black (`#0f172a`) - Represents the stadium at night.
    *   Primary Accent: Electric Lime (`#ccff00`) - High visibility, energetic, common in sports gear.
    *   Secondary Accent: Hot Pink (`#ff00cc`) or Cyan (`#00ccff`) for interactions and highlights.
    *   Text: White for high contrast, dimmed gray for secondary info.
*   **Layout Paradigm**:
    *   **Asymmetric & Dynamic**: Avoid perfect symmetry. Use slanted lines or skewed containers to suggest movement.
    *   **Dashboard-style**: Dense information display, utilizing the full width of the screen (3-column layout).
*   **Signature Elements**:
    *   **Slanted Edges**: Buttons and cards have 45-degree cut corners (clip-path).
    *   **Grid Lines**: Subtle background grid patterns to give a technical feel.
    *   **Glow Effects**: Box-shadows that mimic neon lights on active elements.
*   **Interaction Philosophy**:
    *   **Snappy & Mechanical**: Hover effects are instant and sharp. Buttons "press" down visibly.
    *   **Focus**: Hovering over a card dims the rest of the interface slightly to focus on the content.
*   **Animation**:
    *   **Glitch / Slide**: Content slides in quickly from sides.
    *   **Pulse**: Live indicators or "New" badges pulse.
*   **Typography System**:
    *   **Headings**: `Chakra Petch` or `Teko` (Square, geometric, futuristic).
    *   **Body**: `Roboto` or `Inter` (Clean, readable).
    *   **Numbers**: `JetBrains Mono` or similar monospaced font for stats/scores.
</text>

## Idea 2: "The Locker Room" (Raw & Industrial)

*   **Design Movement**: Brutalism / Industrial Chic
*   **Core Principles**:
    *   **Raw Materials**: Textures like concrete, mesh, or grain.
    *   **Bold & Loud**: Large typography, high contrast, unapologetic layout.
    *   **Authenticity**: Focus on the grit and sweat of sports, not just the polish.
*   **Color Philosophy**:
    *   Background: Light Grey / Concrete (`#f3f4f6`) or textured white.
    *   Primary: Bold Black (`#000000`) for strong lines and text.
    *   Accent: Safety Orange (`#ff5500`) or Signal Blue (`#0044ff`) - utilitarian colors.
*   **Layout Paradigm**:
    *   **Modular / Blocky**: Distinct sections separated by thick black borders.
    *   **Masonry**: Content flows naturally, fitting into available spaces like posters on a wall.
*   **Signature Elements**:
    *   **Thick Borders**: 2px-4px solid black borders on everything.
    *   **Hard Shadows**: No blur shadows, just solid offset blocks (neobrutalism).
    *   **Stenciled Typography**: Use stencil fonts for categories or big numbers.
*   **Interaction Philosophy**:
    *   **Tactile**: Buttons feel like physical switches.
    *   **Direct**: No fading, things appear/disappear instantly or with simple transforms.
*   **Animation**:
    *   **Marquee**: Scrolling text for breaking news or trending tags.
    *   **Stomp**: Elements scale down slightly on click (active state).
*   **Typography System**:
    *   **Headings**: `Oswald` or `Anton` (Tall, condensed, impactful).
    *   **Body**: `Archivo` (Grotesque sans-serif with character).
</text>

## Idea 3: "Stadium Air" (Modern Broadcast)

*   **Design Movement**: Modern TV Broadcast / Clean UI
*   **Core Principles**:
    *   **Clarity & Readability**: Content comes first. UI frames the content without distraction.
    *   **Fluidity**: Smooth transitions, rounded corners, friendly but professional.
    *   **Depth**: Subtle layering to separate content from navigation.
*   **Color Philosophy**:
    *   Background: Dark Navy (`#1e293b`) or Deep Blue-Grey.
    *   Primary: Vivid Blue (`#3b82f6`) or Emerald Green (`#10b981`) - Trustworthy and energetic.
    *   Surface: Slightly lighter panels (`#334155`) with glassmorphism (blur) effects.
*   **Layout Paradigm**:
    *   **Card-Based**: Everything is a card. Clean separation.
    *   **Floating Navigation**: Sticky headers/sidebars that float above the content.
*   **Signature Elements**:
    *   **Glassmorphism**: Translucent backgrounds for headers and overlays.
    *   **Rounded Corners**: `rounded-xl` or `rounded-2xl` for a friendly, modern feel.
    *   **Gradient Accents**: Subtle gradients on buttons or active states.
*   **Interaction Philosophy**:
    *   **Smooth**: Everything eases in and out.
    *   **Feedback**: Ripples on click, gentle scaling on hover.
*   **Animation**:
    *   **Fade & Float**: Elements float up into place.
    *   **Skeleton Loading**: Polished loading states.
*   **Typography System**:
    *   **Headings**: `Montserrat` or `Poppins` (Geometric, modern).
    *   **Body**: `Inter` or `Open Sans` (Neutral).
</text>
